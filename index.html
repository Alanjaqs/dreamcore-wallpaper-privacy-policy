Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 17, 2026

Dreamcore Wallpaper App ("the app") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the app.

1. Information We Do Not Collect Directly

We do not require account registration and we do not directly collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

Favorites, preferences, and settings are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to the developer.

2. Advertising and Third-Party Services

This app uses Google AdMob, a third-party advertising service provided by Google Inc. AdMob may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Advertising ID
  • Device type and model
  • Operating system version
  • Language settings
  • Approximate location (based on IP address)
  • Interaction with advertisements

This information is used to display advertisements and improve ad performance. The developer does not directly access or store this information.

Google's Privacy Policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

3. Personalized Ads and Opt-Out

You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings:

Android:
Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

4. Local Storage

The app may store data locally on your device, such as:

  • Favorite wallpapers
  • App preferences
  • User interface settings

This data remains on your device and is never transmitted externally.

5. Permissions

The app may request permissions necessary for core functionality, such as accessing device storage to apply wallpapers. These permissions are used only for the intended functionality.

6. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed toward children under the age of 13. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

8. Contact

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, you may contact:
